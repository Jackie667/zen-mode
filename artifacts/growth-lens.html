What AI Can't Replace in Growth

  • Brand intuition. Data tells you what people did. Intuition tells you what they'll want next.
  • Creative risk. AI optimises for what worked before. Breakthroughs come from what hasn't been tried.
  • Customer empathy. A review is data. Understanding why someone left a review is human.
  • Ethical guardrails. AI maximises engagement. Humans decide whether that engagement is healthy.
